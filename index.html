The Horqen Legacy

Engineered for Eternity.

Founded on the principles of precision and timeless elegance, Horqen is more than a marketplace; it is a curator of history. We believe that a watch is not merely a tool for measuring time, but a vessel for preserving it.

From the depths of the ocean with the Submariner to the surface of the moon with the Speedmaster, our collection represents the pinnacle of human mechanical achievement.

Authenticity Guaranteed

Every timepiece in our collection undergoes a rigorous multi-point inspection by certified master watchmakers.

Curated Excellence

We do not simply stock watches; we curate icons. Our inventory is hand-selected for rarity, condition, and provenance.

Global Concierge

Our support team operates globally, ensuring a seamless experience from acquisition to delivery, anywhere on Earth.
